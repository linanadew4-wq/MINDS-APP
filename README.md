# MINDS-APP

A mental health mood tracking application that provides emotional support and coping strategies for users experiencing different emotions.

## Features

- 🎯 **Track Your Mood**: Select from three emotional states:
  - Angry
  - Sad
  - Anxious
- 💡 **Get Coping Advice**: Receive personalized strategies tailored to your current mood
- 🎨 **User-Friendly Interface**: Color-coded buttons for easy emotion selection
- 🚨 **Emergency Support**: Quick access to emergency contact information

## How to Use

1. **Start the app** - The home screen displays three mood buttons
2. **Select your mood** - Click on the colored button that matches your current emotion:
   - 🔴 **ANGRY** (coral/red button)
   - 🔵 **SAD** (blue button)
   - 🟢 **ANXIOUS** (green button)
3. **Get advice** - Click anywhere on the emotion screen to cycle through coping strategies
4. **Reset** - Use the reset button to return to the home screen

## Coping Strategies

### 😢 When You're Sad
- Take deep breaths
- Talk to someone you trust
- Write your feelings down
- Go for a short walk

### 😠 When You're Angry
- Count to 10 slowly
- Take a break from the situation
- Squeeze a stress ball
- Drink cold water

### 😰 When You're Anxious
- Name what you can control
- Try grounding yourself: 5-4-3-2-1
- Slow breathing: in 4, out 6
- Stretch your shoulders

## Emergency

**If you're in crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please call 911 immediately.**
